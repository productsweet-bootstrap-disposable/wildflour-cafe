## Summary

Regulars keep missing out on the cabinet by the time they arrive. Explore a lightweight
pre-order-for-pickup flow: choose items the night before, pay online, collect before 9am.

## Target User Or Audience

Weekday commuters who pass the café before 8:30am and want a guaranteed croissant or loaf.

## Requested Capability Or Ask

A simple order form (or third-party integration) covering the cabinet items, with a nightly
cut-off and next-morning pickup window.

## Desired Outcome

Fewer disappointed regulars, better bake planning from overnight demand data.
