## Target User Or Audience

Shoppers buying for someone else

## Requested Capability Or Ask

Ability to purchase and give a gift card (digital or physical) instead of a specific product, so they can let the recipient choose.

## Desired Outcome

Customers can buy a gift card of a chosen denomination, and recipients can redeem it toward a purchase.

## Evidence Or Reproduction Clues

Roughly 4-5 in-person asks a week at the counter, plus two emails last month asking if vouchers are sold. No formal tracking system in place yet — anecdotal counter/email observations only.

## Problem Or Opportunity

Customers are repeatedly asking in-store and via email whether gift cards/vouchers are available, and currently there is no way to sell or redeem one. This is a recurring, unmet demand rather than a one-off request.
